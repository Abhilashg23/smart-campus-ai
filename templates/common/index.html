Our Heritage

About College

Established in 1946, Government Science College (Autonomous) Hassan has grown into a premier institution for higher education. Originally inaugurated by the Maharaja of Mysore, the college was upgraded over the decades and currently accommodates thousands of students across diverse science disciplines. Today, we are proud to be NAAC accredited with a B Grade, holding autonomous status while offering cutting-edge B.Sc. and M.Sc. programs that blend rich heritage with modern innovation.
